Destination

Carmel

Carmel in Batanes is a serene hilltop chapel known for its picturesque setting and traditional Ivatan architecture. Perched gracefully amidst breathtaking landscapes, the chapel offers panoramic views of rolling hills and the ocean. Whether seeking spiritual solace or natural beauty, Carmel promises an unforgettable experience and a haven for photographers and nature lovers alike. Embrace the tranquility and timeless allure of this hidden gem in the northernmost province of the Philippines.

Detailed Travel Guide

Airport

Basco Airport

Basco Airport is the only airport on Batan Island, where Carmel is located. It serves as the main gateway for travelers visiting Batanes. From Basco Airport, you can easily access Carmel and other nearby attractions on Batan Island via various transportation options, such as tricycles or rented vehicles. Keep in mind that the availability of transportation services may vary, so it's advisable to make arrangements in advance or inquire at the airport upon arrival.

Address: CXXG+MC4, Basco, Batanes
Code: BSO
Elevation: 89 m

Journey

Mount Carmel Chapel is found in Batanes, specifically in Basco, Batan Island. To reach Batanes from Manila, travelers can take a 1.5 to 2-hour flight to Basco Airport. From Basco Airport, visitors can access various attractions on Batan Island, including Mount Carmel Chapel, using tricycles or vans.

Accomodations

Basco, being the capital town of Batanes, offers several accommodation options that are not far from Mount Carmel Chapel. Some of the nearest hotels and accommodations to consider are: Fundacion Pacita Nature Lodge: This high-end nature lodge is located on a hill and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscapes. It's about a 10 to 15-minute drive from Mount Carmel Chapel. Shanedel's Inn and Café: Situated in the town proper of Basco, this inn offers comfortable rooms and is approximately a 10 to 15-minute drive from the chapel. Amboy Hometel: Located near the town center, this hometel offers budget-friendly accommodation options and is around a 10 to 15-minute drive from Mount Carmel Chapel. Marfel's Lodge: This lodge is situated in the heart of Basco and provides a cozy stay. It's just a short 10 to 15-minute drive to the chapel. Brandon's Lodge: Another lodging option near the town center, providing basic and comfortable rooms, and it's a 10 to 15-minute drive from Mount Carmel Chapel.

Restaurants

In Basco town, where the popular Mount Carmel Chapel is situated, several dining establishments can be found nearby. These include Bunker's Café, Octagon Bed and Dine, Pension Ivatan Hometel and Restaurant, Beehan's Grill and Restaurant, and Dish Fastfood. Visitors to Mount Carmel Chapel can access these restaurants within a reasonable distance in Basco town. Before visiting, it's advisable to check for updated information on restaurant locations and operating hours.

What's Popular

Here, you'll experience stunning panoramic views of rolling hills and the ocean, capturing perfect photography moments. Immerse yourself in a peaceful and contemplative atmosphere while exploring the cultural charm of Basco town. Don't miss nearby attractions like Vayang Rolling Hills and Naidi Hills, and engage with the friendly Ivatan community to learn about their unique way of life.

Nearest Spots

Batanes offers a wealth of natural wonders and cultural attractions. Besides Mount Carmel Chapel and nearby Vayang Rolling Hills and Naidi Hills, visitors can explore Valugan Boulder Beach, Racuh A Payaman (Marlboro Country), Homoron Blue Lagoon, Dipnaysupuan Japanese Tunnel, Mahatao Boat Shelter Port, Chawa View Deck, and more on Batan Island. Additionally, Sabtang Island features Sabtang Church, Chamantad-Tinyan Viewpoint, Sabtang Lighthouse, and traditional Ivatan villages like Savidug and Chavayan. Immerse yourself in Batanes' unspoiled beauty and rich cultural heritage while planning your itinerary accordingly to make the most of your experience.
