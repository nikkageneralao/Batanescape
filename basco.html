Destination

Basco Lighthouse

Welcome to Basco Lighthouse, a captivating destination nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Batanes, the northernmost province of the Philippines. Rising proudly along the rugged coastline of Basco, the capital town of Batanes, this iconic lighthouse stands as a beacon of both history and beauty. Dating back to the Spanish colonial era, Basco Lighthouse has been a guiding light for sailors and fishermen navigating the treacherous waters of the Luzon Strait. Its stately tower, standing tall at approximately 66 feet, offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the vast ocean and the verdant hills that surround it.

Detailed Travel Guide

Airport

Basco Airport

It is one of two airports in the Batanes Islands, the other being Itbayat Airport. It is located in the provincial capital, Basco.

Address: CXXG+MC4, Basco, Batanes
Code: BSO
Elevation: 89 m

Journey

To travel from Manila to Basco Lighthouse in Batanes, take a 1.5 to 2-hour flight from Ninoy Aquino International Airport or Clark International Airport to Basco Airport. From Basco Airport, hire a tricycle or van to reach Naidi Hills where the Basco Lighthouse is located, which takes about 10 to 15 minutes. Explore the lighthouse, enjoy panoramic views, and immerse yourself in the beauty of the surroundings.

Accomodations

Basco, the location of Basco Lighthouse in Batanes, offers various accommodation options, from budget-friendly inns to luxurious lodges. While there are no hotels directly at Basco Lighthouse, several nearby options provide easy access. These include Fundacion Pacita Nature Lodge, Shanedel's Inn and Café, Amboy Hometel, Marfel's Lodge, and Brandon's Lodge. It's recommended to check for updated listings and book in advance, especially during peak seasons.

Restaurants

Near Basco Lighthouse on Naidi Hills in Basco, Batanes, there are no restaurants or food courts directly on-site. However, several dining options can be found nearby in the town of Basco. Bunker's Café, Octagon Bed and Dine, Pension Ivatan Hometel and Restaurant, Beehan's Grill and Restaurant, and Dish Fastfood are some of the nearest eateries offering a variety of local and Filipino dishes. Visitors can enjoy delicious meals while being within a short distance from Basco Lighthouse, enhancing their overall experience in the area.

What's Popular

Vayang Rolling Hills, located near Basco Lighthouse in Batanes, is a popular and must-visit spot. The area features gently rolling hills with vibrant greenery and stunning views of the ocean. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks or short hikes, capturing beautiful photographs of the picturesque landscape. The peaceful ambiance and captivating beauty make Vayang Rolling Hills a perfect destination to experience the charm of Batanes.p>

Nearest Spots

Batanes, near Basco Lighthouse, offers a range of must-visit attractions for your bucket list. These include Vayang Rolling Hills, Valugan Boulder Beach, Marlboro Country, Tukon Chapel, Naidi Hills, and Sabtang Island. Additionally, explore the Homoron Blue Lagoon, Dipnaysupuan Japanese Tunnel, Mahatao Boat Shelter Port, and the Fountain of Youth (Rapang Cliffs). Embrace the natural wonders, cultural heritage, and stunning landscapes while respecting the local environment and traditions.
